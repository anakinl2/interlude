*Quest Items*
When you hunt monsters assigned as part of a quest, they will occasionally drop quest items. These items do not appear on the ground. They automatically appear in your Inventory, making a distinctive sound. You may check your quest items on the *Quest tab* of the Inventory window (Tab key or ALT+V). If you wish to view ongoing quests, quest items, and quest contents, just click on the Quest button on the bottom-right corner of the screen. (Shortcut Key: ALT+U Close Window
