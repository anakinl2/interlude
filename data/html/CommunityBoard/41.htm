*Last Hero*
Description of Event: Eventa held in the Coliseum. Purpose - to kill as many enemies. For each killed you to give the 5000 multiplied by its level of Aden. Allowed use any objects. Buffs before the fight is not removed. Given 3 minutes to register, 1 minute on the teleport, 5 minutes for fighting. If the winner is determined before the battle ends automatically. After Event, all participants resurrected, healed, and teleported back. In case of death, the experience is not lost. To register for Event, you can use the link (EVENT must be active): [_bbsscripts;41;events.lasthero.LastHero:addPlayer|Register to Event] Your level should correspond to the specified annonse category. If you do not want to participate, but want to watch the battle - you can use reviewing crystals, or to buy seats in the stands at the Arena Director.
