You must also have completed the "Fate's Whisper" quest from Maestro Reorin, you can find in a hut wast of Oren Castle Town. Also, *the level of all your subclasses must have reached 75*. For example, if your current subclasses are Temple Knight and Silver Ranger must have reached 75 if you are to add a third subclass. All characters are limited to *three subclasses*.
