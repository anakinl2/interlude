*[Seal of Avarice]* The Seal of Avarice bestows the following benefits: *Ownership of the Necropolises:* The Lords of Dawn have exclusive rights to the eight Necropolises. In case of a tie, entry is no longer exclusive. *Meeting with Lith:* The door to the Forbidden Sanctum opens and we finally meet Lilith, the Abyssal Mother. She is our greatest enemy, sleeping in the Saints Necropolis. *Commerce with Merchant of Mammon:* You can do business with the Merchant of Mammon in the Necropolises.
