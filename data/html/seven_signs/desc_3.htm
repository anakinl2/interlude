*[What is Seal Stone Collection?]* 1. Collecting seal stones and Seven Signs score: -Collecting seal stones is the most common method of earning points in the Seven Signs competition. The group that collects the most seal stones receives a higher score. -The score a group receives for collecting seal stones will count for 50% of the final score. The remaining 50% of the final score will be determined by the score the group earns during the Festival of Darkness. 2.Collecting seal stones: -To obtain seal stones you must kill Nephilm, Gigant, Lilim and Lith. -After you collect the seal stones, offer them to a Dusk Priestess. Go visit a Dusk Priestess located in each village and use the menu option of “Offer Seal Stones.” -Nephilim, Gigant, Lilim and Lith can only be found in necropolises or catacombs. 3.Collecting seal stones and claiming rewards: -During the Seven Signs competition, if the Revolutionaries of Dusk win, you will receive a reward for collecting seal stones. -The reward will be in proportion to the number of seal stones given to a priestess during the competition, and is paid in the form of ancient adena. You can claim your reward by visiting a Priestess of Dusk, located in each village. [npc_%objectId%_SevenSignsDesc 9|What are necropolises and catacombs?]
