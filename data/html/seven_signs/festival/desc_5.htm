!Festival Guide: *[Festival of Darkness - Bonus Information]* *1. Awarding of Bonuses* Bonuses are awarded to the victors of the competition. They are given in the form of *ancient adena*. Having the top score does not assure the bonus, the party must have also been victorious in battle. If no party in a certain level is eligible for a bonus, the bonus is divided equally between the top scorers of the other levels. *2. Amounts of Bonuses* All parties that participate in the festival must pay a fee. These fees are distributed as bonuses. Naturally, the more parties that participate in the festival, the greater the bonues will be. *3. Bonus Periods* Since some parties challenge the Festival of Darkness more then others, bonuses are not determined until the competition is complete, during the seal validation period. The leader of the highest-scoring victorious party of each level must visit the oracle to recieve the reward. [npc_%objectId%_Chat 0|Go back.]
